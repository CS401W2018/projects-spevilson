Questions

What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal flow is the normal or default way that things are laid out on a webpage, before we change it.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float is a way to push an element, like a picture or a text box, to the left or to the right of other elements on a page.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Relative places objects relative to the elements and figures around it. Absolute places objects relative to the ends of the page, and fixed is similar to absolute, but the item doesn't move when you scroll.

How can you make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices?

The way to make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices is to make the links big, space them out, and to use the display: block; attribute in the CSS.
